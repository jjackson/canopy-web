# Persona: Jonathan Jackson

## Role
Intrapreneurship lead at Dimagi, building the canopy ecosystem and ACE (AI Connect Engine) initiative. Long-time CommCare contributor.

## Day-to-Day
Runs 13+ active projects simultaneously, context-switching constantly between:
- **canopy ecosystem** — canopy (MCP orchestration), canopy-web (this app), canopy-skills, scout, reef
- **ACE/Connect** — ace, ace-web, commcare-connect, commcare-ios, connect-search, connect-website, connect-labs
- **Side projects** — chrome-sales

Builds with Claude Code as a primary tool. Ships fast — multiple PRs/deploys per day. Uses canopy plugins and skills as his personal AI operating system.

## How He Works
- **Decisive** — gives terse answers, doesn't dwell. "B" or "yes" or "C" without explanation.
- **Iterative** — prefers small concrete steps over big plans. Will redirect quickly if something feels off.
- **Skeptical of bloat** — kills features that "wouldn't get used." Cuts speculative columns, removes unnecessary metadata.
- **Trusts his eye** — rejects designs that feel "generic tech dashboard" or "too manual." Wants things to feel "the future."
- **Hands-off about implementation details** — once aligned on the design, says "go for it" and expects clean execution.

## What He Cares About in Tools
- **Information density with clarity** — packed data is good, but it has to be scannable.
- **Practical helpers** — link to live site, link to repo, link to docs. Not aspirational buttons.
- **Shared state across surfaces** — the same project info accessible from web UI, CLI agents, and open claws.
- **AI thinking out loud** — agents that surface insights, not just summaries. But existing tools "aren't quite working" yet.

## What He Doesn't Care About
- Auto-scanned metadata (language, commit counts, sparklines)
- Status fields he sets once and never changes
- Generic visualizations that don't earn their screen space
- Large files of comments or documentation he didn't ask for

## Design Preferences
- **Warm Earth palette** — stone/brown base, warm orange accent. Rejected monochrome and cool blue.
- **DM Sans** font family
- **Dense tile grids** with expand/collapse, not paginated card galleries
- **Read-only by default with edit pencils** — not always-visible textareas

## Communication Style
- Likes Claude to think through tradeoffs proactively but in terse form
- Pushes back when designs feel wrong, even after implementation has started
- Will say "I'm feeling lost" when the conversation has drifted — wants Claude to step back and re-anchor
- Appreciates when Claude reflects back what it's heard and confirms before acting

- Ensuring canopy-web and ace-web converge on the same design system over time
